How it works

  1. Refer the tenant in the portal — tenant, property, tenancy, and your branch. It takes a couple of minutes.
  2. The tenant pays the guarantor fee (one month's rent) through a secure link.
  3. opndoor issues the Deed of Guarantee in favour of the property, and the tenancy completes.

What opndoor is. A professional guarantor service. opndoor provides a Deed of Guarantee in favour of the property; it is not a party to the tenancy agreement, and it is not insurance. If a claim is ever needed, you — the referring agent — are the point of contact.

Who it's for

  • Tenants with no UK-based guarantor.
  • Self-employed, contract or newly-arrived applicants who can afford the rent but don't fit a standard reference.
  • Students, relocators and anyone just under an affordability multiple.
