Management supplement

Analytics, commission, settlement and the admin-only tools — the parts of the portal referrers don't see.

This supplement builds on the core User guide. It covers what management and opndoor admins can see that referrers cannot: estate-wide analytics, the fee and commission economics, the settlement schedule, exports, and — for opndoor admins — reconciliation, the bordereau and the HubSpot sync.

1. Estate-wide analytics

Where a referrer sees only their own referrals, management and opndoor admins see the whole estate. The dashboard's funnel, volume charts and breakdowns aggregate across every partner, agency and branch in view, reframed by the same time-period selector.

Period-clipped breakdown tables are labelled within the selected period so a partial-period figure is never mistaken for a lifetime total.

2. The fee & commission

The guarantor fee is one month's rent per referral. Commission is split from that fee:

PartyShare of the fee
Partner (e.g. the portal partner)25%
Agent (the referring agency)10%

Referrers never see commission. Fee and commission figures are visible to management and opndoor admins only. Referrer-facing performance exports have every partner and agent commission line and column stripped out.

3. Settlement schedule

Commission settles on a monthly cycle:

  • Each settlement covers the prior calendar month.
  • It is calculated net of refunds — a referral refunded in the month is removed from the total.
  • It is payable on the 15th of the following month.

The settlement view gives one figure per partner, with the constituent applications behind it.

4. The referrer league

The League ranks referrers by performance. What each referrer sees on their own leaderboard is controlled from Manage partner — that is the single lever for leaderboard visibility, and changing it prompts a confirmation because it changes what every referrer at that partner sees.

5. Exports

  • Performance export — the analytics for the selected period, as a branded workbook. Referrer-tier exports omit all commission lines and columns.
  • Applications / CSV — the underlying records for the current view.

opndoor admin only. The premium bordereau export — the underwriter's monthly schedule — is restricted to opndoor superadmins. It is produced verbatim to the underwriter template (grouped headers, the fixed column order, the premium rate in the header block, and the premium amount per policy).

6. Managing users

Management can add their own users. Each user has a role — management or referrer — that sets what they can see. Role descriptions on the user screen describe the partner-facing roles without exposing opndoor-internal admin language.

7. opndoor admin tools

These tools are visible to opndoor admins only; management and referrers never see them.

Reconciliation

Agencies and branches created on the fly by referrers land here for review. Confirm one as a new canonical record, or (in a later release) merge a likely duplicate. A single-office agency's automatic "Head office" branch folds into the agency card — confirming the agency confirms both — and the queue counts decisions, not raw rows.

HubSpot sync

Confirmed agencies and branches sync to HubSpot automatically every couple of minutes; confirming a record also nudges an immediate sync, and there is a manual Sync HubSpot button. Portal-minted companies carry their commission rate across. The sync is one-way and never touches HubSpot-owned finance, owner or attribution fields.

Resetting a user's authenticator

An opndoor superadmin can reset a user's two-factor authenticator. It clears the enrolment (audited), and the user is prompted to enrol again at their next sign-in.
